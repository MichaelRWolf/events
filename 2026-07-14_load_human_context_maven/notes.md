## Context

Free lightning lesson on managing cognitive load in AI agent workflows. Teaches how to configure agent briefing formats to match individual preferences and reduce information overload. Core challenge: agents often generate excessive output (getting "fifteen paragraphs" from "three sentences" of input).

- Reducing information overload in agent-generated content
