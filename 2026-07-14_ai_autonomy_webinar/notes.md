## Key Concepts

The Five Levels of AI Autonomy framework will be covered, with focus on:

- **Conversational AI (Level 2)** -- Exploring ambiguous requirements
- **Task Agents (Level 3)** -- Automated, well-defined work (e.g., test suite development)
- Real-world problem mapping across all five autonomy levels
- Core principle: As AI handles more work autonomously, the quality of human intent and clear task definition become the limiting factors
